About SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is an animated comedy series set in Bikini Bottom. It follows SpongeBob, a cheerful sea sponge who works at the Krusty Krab and lives in a pineapple under the sea.

The show features characters like Patrick Star, Squidward Tentacles, Mr. Krabs, and Sandy Cheeks. It is known for surreal humor, slapstick comedy, and chaotic underwater storytelling.

Since 1999, it has become one of the most iconic animated series in television history.
